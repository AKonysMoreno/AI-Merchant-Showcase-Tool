Get oriented

Case studies are a top request from Shopify partners, and for good reason! They're a great way to show your value in working with Shopify merchants. But case studies also take a bit of time to build, and sometimes you need social proof faster.

That's where this merchant showcase tool comes in.

This tool is a low-lift way to build content that shows the value of your work with merchants on Shopify. It includes the same impactful value points and details about the work you did as a full case study, but in a more bite-sized format.

The output is a social post featuring a carousel of polished content cards you can promote through LinkedIn.

Reminder: Make sure you have your merchant's approval of your content before posting. Shopify is not liable for any content created using this tool.

Guidelines

Build your content, and own it.

This resource is entirely on you! Shopify won't need to review it, nor will we promote this content on our channels.

Give the details your all.

There are only three elements needed to create merchant showcase content. This content type is designed to have the highest impact with the lowest effort, but you'll need to include all elements for it to be effective. Please don't cut corners!

Keep your merchants in the loop.

We're simply providing this template as a resource. You'll be responsible for making sure that any merchants you feature have your approval.

Post on LinkedIn

Once you've created your content cards in Figma and exported them as PDFs, follow these actions to build your post and share on LinkedIn.

Action 1: Input this prompt into your AI tool.

Now you need to draft a one sentence description for a LinkedIn post that will showcase this case study. I'd like you to study these examples and learn their voice and tone. You'll then want to write the new LinkedIn body copy sentence in a similar style. <Example 1> Don't let fragmented systems slow you down. Learn how we helped Aviator Nation unify their online and in-store channels on Shopify. <Example 2> Shed the limitations of your legacy platform. Learn how we migrated Audio Advice to Shopify, building intuitive product discovery tools for complex technology purchases. <Example 3> Manage multiple brands without losing what makes each unique. Learn how we helped Lids transform their multi-brand ecosystem and build bespoke customization features on Shopify. <Example 4> Don't lose your brand identity because of rigid technology. Learn how we helped Chaz Dean bring their signature consultative approach online by migrating to Shopify. <Example 5> High performance products call for innovative shopping experiences. Learn how we helped Superfeet build guided selling tools and localize across global markets on Shopify. Applying what you've learned from these examples, take the earlier input for "What's the single most important thing that you want to convey about the work you did with this brand?" and turn it into the body copy for the post. Don't exceed 175 characters.

Action 2: Build your post on LinkedIn.

  1. Navigate to LinkedIn
  2. Click "Start a post" on your profile
  3. In the post editor, click the "+" (plus) icon to find more post options
  4. Select "Add a document"
  5. Upload your PDF files in this card order: Title, Summary, Image, Stats, Quote, Logos
  6. Add "[Merchant name] x [Your agency's name] showcase" as the title for the document
  7. Paste in the caption you generated in your AI tool
  8. Click "Post" to share with your network
